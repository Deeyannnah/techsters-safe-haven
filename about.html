About Safe Haven

An organisation that strives for a just world by advancing the girl child rights and equality for females.

Safe Haven supports women and girls who have experienced or are suffering from female genital mutilation (FGM) and Child or Forced marriages (CFM). Our solution focuses on the girl child's traumas, especially those influenced by tradition and ancestral beliefs.

We are active in Africa and work in partnership with children, families, supporters, community-based organizations and governments. Together, we help ensure that the girl child can access their rights to:

  • Safety
  • Education
  • Health care
  • Well being
  • Dignity
  • Protection in emergencies

How we create a
lasting impact

We work with children, young people, supporters and partners to tackle the root causes of barriers facing the girl child.

We support girls' rights from birth to adulthood

We enable children and communities to prepare for and respond to crises and adversity

We drive change in practice and policy at all levels using our reach, experience and knowledge

Our Vision and Mission

  • To become a ray of hope for the victims
  • To establish contact and be the victim's first call in case of danger.
  • To offer a place of comfort to all victims.

Our Mandate

Focus 1: We seek to enlighten people on the dangers of female genital mutilation and child marriage.

Focus 2: We seek to reduce violence against women, in and outside marriages.

Focus 3: We strive to provide a place of solace to victims, becoming their first point of contact during danger.
